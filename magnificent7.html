2026 Skepticism Outlook: Three Scenarios

Bull Case: Skepticism Eases

AGI timeline predictions (late 2026-early 2027) are met. NVIDIA continues exceeding expectations. Enterprise AI adoption inflection arrives. AI revenue growth starts closing the gap with investment.

Base Case: "Splinter" Scenario

Market bifurcates between "monetizers" (companies using AI to generate revenue) and "manufacturers" (building AI infrastructure at massive cost). Winners and losers diverge sharply. Overall skepticism remains elevated but concentrated.

Bear Case: Skepticism Intensifies

The 100x revenue gap remains unbridged. Free cash flow compression forces difficult shareholder conversations. An OpenAI funding crisis or major AI project failure becomes a catalytic event. Valuation compression across the sector.

Bottom Line

AI skepticism has evolved from a contrarian view to a mainstream institutional concern. The fundamental tension — between AI's transformative potential and the extraordinary financial risks of the current investment cycle — remains unresolved. The 2026-2028 window is widely viewed as the critical period where the bull and bear cases will be tested against reality. The market is shifting from rewarding AI promises to demanding AI profits, and the Magnificent 7's diverging performance reflects exactly this transition.
