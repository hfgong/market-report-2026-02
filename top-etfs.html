Sector Rotation Themes for 2026

The shift is clear: Small-cap value (+5.9% YTD) is outpacing large-cap growth (+0.1% YTD). As rate cuts continue and the yield curve steepens, cyclical sectors and small caps benefit from lower debt costs. The Magnificent 7's dominance is cracking — the broadening has arrived.

Active vs. Passive ETFs — The Great Shift

Active ETFs now outnumber passive ETFs by count (Bloomberg). They captured ~37-40% of January 2026 flows despite holding only ~11% of total AUM (ETF Trends). SEC approval of ETF share classes (late 2025) will further accelerate this trend (Morningstar).
